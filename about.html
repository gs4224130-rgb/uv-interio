ABOUT UV INTERIO

Design feels better
when it feels real.

A local showroom built around materials you can touch, lights you can experience, and choices you can compare before you commit.

OUR STORY

From a wall finish to a full mood.

UV INTERIO brings together PVC panels, WPC louvers, UV sheets, charcoal panels, chandeliers, decorative lamps and designer lighting in one showroom near Jain Honda on Alwar Road, Tijara.

The goal is simple: make selection easier by letting customers compare real finishes, colours and lighting effects together.

OWNER

Gourav Singh

Focused on helping customers turn scattered inspiration into a clear, practical direction for their interiors.

Bring a photo of your room, a colour you like, or even a rough idea — the showroom experience is designed to help you narrow the choices.
